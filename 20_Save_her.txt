“Stick out your hand! I nee-” You cough and sputter as sand gets in your mouth. “I need to pull you out!” 

You dig through the giant sand pile, and suddenly feel fangs bite deep into your arm and slither away. 

“Ah shit!” You shake it off and grab hold of the girl’s hand. 

You begin to pull her out from under the sand, grunting and struggling, and eventually manage to move her onto your back.

“Got ya.” You trudge through the pouring sand holding onto Aelita with one hand and her heart in the other. 

Finally making it to the doorway, it takes you a few tries to push through the doorway’s seemingly magical field before falling into the hallway.
