You open your satchel to try to put the tome inside, but a force is denying it entry. 

“What the heck?” 

Confused, you try to push it into the bag again, a magical force denied entry and tossed the book out of your hand and onto the altar.  

A gust of wind opens the tome and flips the pages wildly. 

The pages stop and the dusty wind begins to wrap around your body, trying to pull you into the book.

“No no no no no...” 

You grunt and try as hard as you can to hold onto the pulpit as the suction becomes more powerful, but your grip slips. 

You are sucked up into the air and down into the book. 

The book snaps shut and the whirlwind dies down.

You are trapped.

-The End
