Your heart jumps inside your chest as you turn and run, you see an open door on your right. 

Your only chance. 

You hear a roar behind you and a rush of wind at your back, that suddenly switches and tries to pull you backwards. 

“Shit shit shit!” you said. 

So close to the door, you grab onto the doorway and pull yourself through. 

The wind stops and you see the dark mist rush against the doorway. 

It fights pushing and battering against the doorway but it seems it cannot enter this room. 

You coil your whip and set it on your hip. 

You see a few rows of pews on either side of the room and a big altar with a giant, faded, golden symbol made out of wood.
