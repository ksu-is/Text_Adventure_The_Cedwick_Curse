You do not remember how you got here. 

You have no memories beyond this point. 

Though you feel very smart, and have a knack for adventure and archeology. 

“Wh-where am I?"

The white figure from before stands in front of you walks towards you.

You unfurl your whip but the figure doesn't stop. 

Sweat rolls down your back, you become nervous, searching for an exit.

“I need your help, my family has been taken away from me, 
turned into monsters, but you… you can save us, please.“ 

The ghostly white figure comes into a clearer appearance. 

A little girl, no more than the age of twelve. 

Her hair is curled tightly and gently bounces as she floats towards you. 

She is no taller than your waist.  

“Please help me.”
