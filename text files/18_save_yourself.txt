As a snake starts to coil up your leg, you pay no attention to the girl.  

Screaming, you frantically run towards the exit of the room, a heavy door.

The snake falls off of your leg and you manage to push the door open, amidst the poor girl screaming for help. 

You keep running to the left and find another passageway and lean against the wall, slumping down to sit and pant heavily.

“Damn it.” You feel the heart beating faster and faster in your hand and quickly throw it into the bag. “Fuck that’s creepy.” 

The blood on your hand is wiped off onto the bag. 

You put your head in your hands, panting as your heart beat slows down. 

“I have to get out of here.”
