The wailing shakes the walls, getting closer and closer, louder and louder.

You see Aelita disappear through the wall and a moment later you rush through the doorway into the room with the giant golden, carved symbol.

“M-y heart! Put it on the pulpit!” she shouts.

Both of you rush towards the altar, past the pews as a loud bang and cracking sound came from the doorway.  

The ghastly creature was back and battered itself against the doorway, seemingly unable to get through. 

You hear Aelita grunt and yelp as you place the girl’s heart on the pulpit.  

You can see that her heart is beating quickly and slowly, draining of color.

“Quick! Indy! Open the book to the page that says October 30th, 1903 and read what's there!” She yelled over the noise, her voice panicked and in pain.

“B..but what about you? Is… it hurting you? I mean… you..your heart?”

“N-no!.. I mean, yes, but you can break the curse, just read this passage! You’re the man with the whip, aren't you?!”
