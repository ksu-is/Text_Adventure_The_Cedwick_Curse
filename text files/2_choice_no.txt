You jump back, “No! Stay back!” 

You trip over a branch. 

“I gotta get out of here.”

You get up and start to run, faster and faster.  

The rain was coming down so hard that you could not see anything and the wind blew harder, nearly sweeping you off your feet.

Amidst the storm and your panic, you managed to run past the gates and into the street. 

An eighteen-wheeler barreled down the road, frantically looking for a place to pull off. 

You barely dodge the truck and run down the street away from the mansion.

You have survived for now.

The End.
