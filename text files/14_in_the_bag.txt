Its skeletal-like hand reached out from the smokey abyss.

“Just what I needed…” It opened the book, followed by a loud shrieking and a bright white light from the book.

In its wake, a shelf dropped to the floor taking its contents with it. A shattering of glass and plumes of gases emanate from the remnants of the shelf, except one jar.
