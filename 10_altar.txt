“Might as well check that altar.”

You pick yourself up, dust your clothes off the best you can and make your way over to the structure.

In front of you is an intricately carved altar, that once golden trim had turned to a reddish rust from years of aging.  

You search along the side of it hoping to find a drawer, a hidden compartment, but to no avail. 

“Maybe the pulpit.” 

You walk up the stairs and start to feel a force pressing down on you, just like before but not nearly as strong.  

You carefully climb the creaky stairs and reach the pulpit. 

You find a drawer and pull it open, a cloud of dust rises from it. 

Coughing, you find an ancient looking tome.
