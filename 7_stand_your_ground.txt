You decide to stand and fight, you whip at the mist, but does nothing to stop the relentless assault. 

You start to run and see an open door to your right. You feel something curl around your ankle and pull you back. 

“No!” you shout, as you grab onto the doorway. 

Grunting, you try to pull yourself into the room; however, the struggle is too much, you are pulled back into the mist. 

You valiantly try to grab at the floor boards, not wanting to surrender. 

You catch a board with your left hand, you turn onto your back, and try to whip at the mist with your right.

*crack*

The black mist slowly crawls up your leg an immense amount of pressure on your leg, pressing down and pulling you in. 

The mist continues moving up your body as you scream in agony feeling like 10 times earth’s gravity sprawling up your body. 

The board you were holding snaps, finalizing your demise.

You are pulled into the dark, black void, never to be seen again.
There is nothing left of your body. 
You have died.

-The End
