To your right, the little ghost girl appeared through a wall and began to untie your bindings. 

“You saved me, right? That was you?” You said to the girl. As you hold your head and grab the book from the floor, and open it. 

On the inside cover, it read:

 “Aelita Cedwick. Age 9.”

Time slows down as you begin to skim the book.  

Not much is there, no life experiences, 

Just a notebook with doodles and drawings of a life yearned, 

her parents with her, 

a dog, 

playing dress up.

You close it as tears welled up in your eyes.  

Her voice snaps your back to reality.
