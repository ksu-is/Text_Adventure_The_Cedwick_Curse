You slowly come to as you hear a rattling of chains and your limbs being pulled in different ways, before you pass out again.

You feel weaker than before as your snap to reality.

You lay on a table, strapped down by spectral chains. 

You are awake but unable to get up.

Your satchel lays on the floor next to you as your eyes shift around. 

Its a dark room, stained dark red and brown blood-stained tiles cover the walls.

There are shelves along the wall with jars on which they sit. 

Some of the jars are filled with sand, dirt and some organs floating in a green ooze.

Suddenly, a dark wispy figure, a shade, appears above you. It speaks in a soft echoing voice, 

“Wheeerreee isss the toooome?”
