You rest and regain your breath on the floor, coughing and spitting up sand that got in your mouth.

Aelita gasped for air, as if forgetting that she needed to breathe.  

The beating heart slips from your hand and feels like a weight has been lifted off your back.

“Thank you, for saving me.”

“So… you must be Aelita?” You ask after a little while, sitting up, and notice the girl has gone incorporeal, like before.

“I..Yes, I am. Aelita Cedwick.” 

“And now you’re a ghost….again?”, you ask.

“Yep, this is me.”

“So, I could have dropped your heart in the bag, and you would have returned to this state?” You sigh and hold pressure on your wound.

“Mmmhm.....sorry ” You see her rock back and forth in the air, sheepishly. “Sooo, whats your name?”

“Its…its fine. Oh… I guess my name is Indy…." You take a moment, wondering why you can't remember.

"Anyways, so about your curse, can it really be broken? Also… you’re quite young too, is this how old you were when it happened?”

“Well Indy… I think so, I mean, that old witch cursed my family, and you match the prophecy. I was only nine when it happened, so I don't really know…”

“So.. you didn't get to live… really, didn't get to experience life, huh?”

“I guess not.”

An echoing wail shook the hallway, floorboards creaked against the nails that held them down.. 

“Ooookay, time to go. I can heal you in the altar room.”

Both of you rush towards the altar room.
