Suddenly, you feel yourself falling, as shapes form around you and you fall flat on your face, on a wooden and cracked floor. 

You groan and slowly stand up, holding your head. 

Before you is a tattered hallway. 

Picture frames line the walls, some torn and most have been warped, the frames broken and faded from their once golden appearance.

Your sopping wet clothes magically transform into a pair of brown cargo pants, 

a light brown long sleeve shirt with pockets on either side, 

a leather belt and a brown bullwhip lay curled on the floor in front of you. 

You suddenly feel that you really hate snakes; however, you now have a weapon, a Bullwhip, and a sudden thirst for adventure.  

You remember your name, Indy.
