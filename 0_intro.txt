
There was once a house that belonged to a Steel Baron family in the early 1900s, the Cedwicks.  

As the industrial revolution in America boomed, their steel business grew and grew and grew.

As their wealth grew, however, so did their greed. The Cedwick family had Frank, Jane, and Aelita their daughter.

They had maids and landscapers take care of Aleita, while Frank and Jane went away on vacation after vacation.

The family grew up well-off, until Frank and Jane broke ground on their next factory on an ancient burial ground on Hallow’s Eve. 

“You have trespassed on our land and tarnished our sacred place.” The witch doctor screamed at Frank, 
“Forever and on you shall never die. Until a man with a fear of snakes comes, you will never move on!” 

The witch doctor raised her arms, swirled her staff around and lightning crashed down from the sky and struck Frank and Jane Cedwick.

Nothing was left of them. Aelita inherited the business but died of a broken heart. 

Her family roamed their ill-fated house as specters, desperate to save what they had left of their ‘fortune’.

Aelita knew there had to be a way to break this curse. 

She began to call out to trick-or-treaters every year on Hallow’s eve, in hopes that one could come to lift their curse.
